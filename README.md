**# 📊 Sales Performance & Profitability Analysis Dashboard**

🚀 End-to-End Power BI Project | Data Modeling | DAX | Business Insights

---

**## 📌 Overview
**
This project analyzes sales and profitability across multiple dimensions including product, region, customer, and time. The goal is to identify key business drivers and inefficiencies affecting profitability.

---

**## 🎯 Business Problem**

Despite strong sales performance, profitability is inconsistent. This project aims to identify:

* Unprofitable products
* Impact of discounts on profit
* High-performing regions and categories

---

**## 🛠️ Tools Used**

* Power BI
* DAX
* Excel

---

**## 📊 Key KPIs**

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin
* Year-over-Year Growth
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Sales per Customer
* Unprofitable Products

**## 🔍 Key Insights**
